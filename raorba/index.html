About Us:

About the Conference

International Conference on Recent Advances in Operations Research & Business Analytics (RAORBA-2024) is an event that serves as a focal point for researchers, academicians, practitioners, and industry experts in the fields of operations research and management. Typically spanning several days, the conference gathers participants from diverse backgrounds to exchange ideas, share insights, and discuss the latest advancements and challenges in the different fields. Each iteration of the conference revolves around a specific theme, guiding the selection of keynote speakers, session topics, and paper submissions. Renowned experts and thought leaders are invited to deliver keynote speeches, offering valuable perspectives on current research trends, industry best practices, and future directions in the different field of operations research and management.
One of the highlights of the conference is the presentation of research papers, which undergo a rigorous peer-review process. Accepted submissions are grouped into thematic sessions, allowing presenters to showcase their work and engage in meaningful discussions with fellow attendees. In addition to academic presentations, the conference program may include invitee talk, keynote talk, covering a wide range of topics relevant to operations research and business analytics. These sessions foster interdisciplinary collaboration and offer practical insights into real-world applications and challenges.
Networking plays a crucial role in the conference experience, with various opportunities for participants to connect during coffee breaks, lunches, receptions, and social events. These informal interactions facilitate the exchange of ideas, the formation of collaborations, and the building of professional relationships across academia and industry. For authors, presenting at the RAORBA-2024 conference can lead to publication opportunities in special issues of journals or edited volumes, extending the impact of their research and contributing to the scholarly discourse in the field.

About the Venue- Bharatiya Vidya Bhavan Institute of Management Science, Kolkata

Bharatiya Vidya Bhavan Institute of Management Science, Kolkata, India was started in the year 2000 under the aegis of Bharatiya Vidya Bhavan as a promising B-School under Maulana Abul Kalam Azad University of Technology (MAKAUT). Here, MBA program is affiliated to the MAKAUT and is approved by the All India Council for Technical Education (AICTE) and BBA program is affiliated to the MAKAUT, Ministry of Human Resource Development, Govt. Of India and Department of Higher education (Technical Education), Govt. Of West Bengal. The Institute conducts a 2-year MBA program and 3-year BBA program on a regular basis.

At present, we offer a specialization in four areas, Marketing, Human Resources, Finance. To make the students better equipped to handle the ever-changing business scenario, dual specialization is now offered. Students are required to select 3 courses from one area (Major Specialization) and one course from a different area (Minor Specialization).

For more details, visit our website: https://bimskol.org/

Operational Research Society of India (ORSI)

The Society is devoted to promotion and propagation of knowledge in Operational Research. ORSI publishes a quarterly journal OPSEARCH, which brings out high quality and state of the art papers in Operational Research. Since 1973, it also conducts an examination on Graduate Diploma in Operational Research to provide opportunities to professionals and students to equip themselves with knowledge of Operational Research. Operational Research Society of India is a non-profit making educational Society.

For more details, visit our website: https://orsihq.org/

Objective of the Conference:

Foster collaboration and networking among professionals from academia, industry, and government sectors.

Facilitate the dissemination of cutting-edge research findings and innovative methodologies in operations research and business analytics.

Promote interdisciplinary approaches and cross-disciplinary collaborations to address complex real-world problems.

Provide a forum for discussing emerging trends, best practices, and future directions in the field.

Encourage the integration of theoretical insights with practical applications to enhance decision-making processes and organizational performance.

Support the development of young researchers and students by offering opportunities for presenting their work, receiving feedback, and engaging with established experts in the field.
